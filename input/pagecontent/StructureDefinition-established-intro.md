
**Example of extension**

```

<Location xmlns="http://hl7.org/fhir">
 ...
  <extension url="http://hl7.org.nz/fhir/StructureDefinition/established">
    <valuePeriod>
      <start value="2010-01-01"/>
    </valuePeriod>
  </extension>
  <name value="Daves health centre"/>
  <alias value="Best health"/>
  ...
</Location>

```
